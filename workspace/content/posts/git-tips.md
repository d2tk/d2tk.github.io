---
title: "How to Exclude a Folder in .gitignore"
date: "2025-01-12"
type: "article"
tags: ["GIT_TIPS"]
---

To exclude a folder in `.gitignore`, append a forward slash (`/`) to the folder name.

> Example:
> 
> ```
> logs/
> node_modules/
> ```

This ensures that the entire folder and its contents are ignored by Git.
